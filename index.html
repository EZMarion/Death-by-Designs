About Author

Hello, I'm a writer from Myanmar who has always been fascinated by the world of crime and mystery. Ever since I was young, I loved watching detective movies—the suspense, the secrets, and the thrill of uncovering the truth. As I grew older, my passion expanded to include mystery, thriller, and dark fantasy. I began creating my own characters and imagining twisted plots filled with hidden motives and chilling truths.
