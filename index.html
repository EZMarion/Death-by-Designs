Death by Designs

by E. Z. Marion

Start Reading
#Action #Detective #Mystery #Thriller #Religion

This is my very first piece of writing and my own original novel, not a translation. This novel is set in the 1900s and is especially suitable for those who enjoy decetive stories,action, and mystery. Kindly forgive me if I did anything wrong.When a high prince, a weary investigator, and a cold-eyed bounty hunter cross paths, they uncover a chilling truth buried beneath aristocratic lies, revenge, and forgotten justice. But the real terror is not the crimes themselves—it's who dares to carry them out, and why.

About Author

Hello, I'm a writer from Myanmar who has always been fascinated by the world of crime and mystery. Ever since I was young, I loved watching detective movies — the suspense, the secrets, and the thrill of uncovering the truth. As I grew older, my passion expanded to include mystery, thriller, and dark fantasy. I began creating my own characters and imagining twisted plots filled with hidden truths. "Death by Designs" is my very first novel — an original story born from my imagination. I’m excited to share my ideas and characters with you, and I hope you’ll enjoy walking into the dark, intriguing world I’ve built. Thank you for reading — and welcome to the mystery.
